About Jordan

Hello, my name is Jordan O'Leary.

My beginning into tattooing was one of more curiosity than anything else. From the moment I put ink into skin I became obsessed and fascinated with the art of tattooing. I have been tattooing for over two years, specialising in graphic and saturated realism. I have spent the vast majority of my time studying realistic portraiture and how best to translate it to skin. Creating tattoos that heal cleanly and stand the test of time remains my primary focus.

Welcome to my private studio in Carrigtwohill, Co. Cork. I look forward to creating something unique for you.
